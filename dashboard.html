Frequently Asked Questions

How does MyMilesAI track my drives?
MyMilesAI uses your phone's GPS and motion sensors to automatically detect when you're driving. It logs every trip in the background - no need to press start or stop.
What's the IRS mileage rate for 2026?
The IRS standard mileage rate for 2026 is $0.725 per mile for business use. For Canada, the CRA rate is $0.73/km for the first 5,000 km and $0.67/km after that.
Can I use MyMilesAI for tax deductions?
Yes! MyMilesAI generates IRS-compliant reports with drive date, purpose, start/end locations, and total mileage value. Export as CSV or PDF for your tax filing.
How do I cancel my subscription?
Go to Subscription in the sidebar, scroll to Cancel Subscription, and click Cancel. Your data is preserved and you can reactivate anytime.
Is my data secure?
Your data is encrypted and stored on Supabase (powered by PostgreSQL with Row Level Security). Only you can access your drives. We never share or sell your data.
