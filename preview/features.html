Features

Everything you need. Nothing you don't.

From automatic GPS tracking to IRS-ready reports, MyMilesAI handles every part of mileage tracking so you can focus on driving your business.

Auto GPS Tracking

Detects driving automatically using motion sensors and GPS. Every trip logged in the background with distance, route, time, and speed. No buttons, no app switching — just drive.

AI Classification

Learns your routes and auto-classifies every trip as business, personal, medical, or charity. One-swipe override any time. Accuracy improves the more you drive.

Real-Time Deductions

Watch your IRS or CRA deduction tick up live as you drive. Know exactly how much you're saving at every moment — $0.725/mile (US) or $0.73/km (Canada).

IRS & CRA Reports

Export tax-compliant mileage logs as PDF or CSV. Email them to your accountant in one tap. Supports Schedule C, Form 2106, T2200, and T777 requirements.

Live Map & Routes

Watch trips unfold on a real-time map. Review routes, visualize patterns, and verify distances with precision. Full route history preserved for audits.

Privacy First

All trip data stored on your device by default. No cloud uploads, no third-party tracking, no ads. Your location is yours alone. Delete anything, anytime.

Privacy

Your data. Your device.

Unlike other mileage apps that upload every trip to their cloud, MyMilesAI stores your GPS coordinates, routes, and trip history locally on your phone. Nothing is uploaded unless you explicitly sync to the web dashboard — and even then, only metadata (date, distance, category) syncs. Your actual routes never leave the device.
