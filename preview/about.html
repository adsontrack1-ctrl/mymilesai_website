About

We built the mileage tracker we wanted.

MyMilesAI is made by people who drive a lot, hate tracking mileage, and refuse to hand their GPS data to ad networks.

Our mission

To give drivers a fair, private, and accurate way to claim every dollar they've earned in tax deductions — without selling their location data, cluttering their phone with ads, or forcing them through a 20-minute setup.

Why we started

Every mileage app on the market was missing something. The big ones uploaded your entire driving history to their servers and sold it to insurance companies. The cheap ones missed half your trips. The "privacy-focused" ones cost $15/month and had terrible UX.

So we built MyMilesAI — on-device tracking, automatic classification, accountant-ready reports, at a fair price. No ads. No data harvesting. No beta tax.

Who we are

MyMilesAI is operated by Harijas LLC, an independent software company. We're small, focused, and self-funded — no VCs, no pressure to maximize engagement metrics or sell user data. Our only goal is to make MyMilesAI the best mileage tracker for self-employed drivers in North America.
