Download & set up

Install MyMilesAI from the App Store. Enter your name, choose miles or kilometres, and grant location permission. You'll be done in under 30 seconds.

Just drive

MyMilesAI detects when you're driving using motion sensors and low-power GPS. Every trip is logged automatically with distance, route, time, and speed — no buttons to press, no apps to switch.

  • ✓ Works offline — no signal needed
  • ✓ Less than 5% extra battery per day
  • ✓ AI classifies business vs. personal
3

Claim your deductions

At tax time, export an IRS or CRA-ready mileage log as PDF or CSV. Business miles are calculated at the current standard rate — $0.725/mile for the US, $0.73/km for the first 5,000 km in Canada.

  • ✓ One-tap email to your accountant
  • ✓ CSV, Excel, Google Sheets export
  • ✓ Schedule C, Form 2106, T2200 ready
