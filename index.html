The Smartest Way to Track Business Mileage in 2026

MyMilesAI is a mile tracker app built for self-employed professionals, freelancers, 1099 contractors, gig workers, real estate agents, delivery drivers, rideshare drivers, and anyone who drives for business. The app automatically tracks every trip using GPS, classifies drives as business or personal using AI, and calculates your tax deductions in real time based on the current IRS standard mileage rate ($0.725 per mile for 2026) or the CRA automobile allowance rate ($0.73 per kilometre in Canada).

Unlike other mileage trackers, MyMilesAI is built with a privacy-first approach. All trip data, GPS coordinates, routes, work mileage records, and odometer readings are stored locally on your device. Nothing is uploaded to the cloud. The app works fully offline, tracks trips in low-signal areas, and never requires an internet connection to start logging a drive. Your business expense data stays on your phone.

MyMilesAI generates IRS-compliant and CRA-compliant mileage logs that can be exported as PDF or CSV files and shared directly with your accountant. These reports support Schedule C filings, tax write-off documentation, and mileage reimbursement claims. The built-in mileage calculator shows your deductions in real time. Features include work-hours auto-classification, swipe-to-classify gestures, Smart Pause GPS logic, and undo-delete for accidentally removed trips. Your daily commute is automatically excluded from business mileage calculations.

The app offers a free tier with 20 trips per month and a Pro plan at $6.99 per month with unlimited trips, auto GPS tracking, AI classification, and full export capabilities. During the current beta period, all Pro features are available for free via Apple MyMilesAI. MyMilesAI supports both the United States and Canada with automatic locale detection, making it the ideal business expense tracker for professionals in both countries.

For questions or support, contact [email protected]. MyMilesAI is available as a free beta on iOS via MyMilesAI, with Android support coming soon.
