FAQ

Does it drain my battery?+
We use low-power GPS and intelligent motion detection. Most users report less than 5% additional battery drain per day.
How accurate is the GPS tracking?+
Accuracy within 2–3% of actual distance, meeting IRS mileage log requirements.

All trip data is stored locally on your device. We never upload your location, sell data, or serve ads.
