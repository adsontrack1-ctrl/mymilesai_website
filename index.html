FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Does it drain my battery?+
We use low-power GPS and intelligent motion detection. Most users report less than 5% additional battery per day.
Is my location data private?+
All trip data is stored locally on your device. We never upload your location, sell data, or serve ads.
Does it work offline?+
Yes — MyMilesAI tracks and stores trips entirely on your device. No signal? No problem.
What are the 2026 mileage rates?+
The 2026 IRS rate is $0.725/mile. In Canada, the CRA rate is $0.73/km for the first 5,000 km and $0.67/km after.
