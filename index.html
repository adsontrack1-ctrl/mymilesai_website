FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Does it drain my battery?+
We use low-power GPS and intelligent motion detection. Most users report less than 5% additional battery per day.
How accurate is the GPS tracking?+
MyMilesAI uses native GPS with road-snapping for accuracy within 2–3% of actual distance — well within IRS mileage log requirements.
Is my location data private?+
All trip data is stored locally on your device. We never upload your location, sell data, or serve ads. GPS permissions can be revoked at any time.
Does it work offline?+
Yes — MyMilesAI tracks and stores trips entirely on your device. No signal? No problem.
What are the 2026 mileage rates?+
The 2026 IRS rate is $0.725/mile for business. In Canada, the CRA rate is $0.73/km for the first 5,000 km and $0.67/km after.
How do I cancel?+
Cancel anytime through the App Store, Google Play, your account settings, or by emailing support@mymilesai.com. No fees, no penalties.
What's the refund policy?+
30-day money-back guarantee. Email support@mymilesai.com within 30 days for a full refund — no questions asked.
